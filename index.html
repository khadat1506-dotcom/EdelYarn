Our story

Tailoring without compromise.

EdelYarn was founded on a simple belief: a custom-made garment shouldn't be a privilege reserved for the few. We pair Swiss-guided measurements and styling with the craftsmanship of master tailors in Vietnam, bringing together two traditions that have, for generations, set the standard for precision and patience.

Swiss precision

Measured in Switzerland.

Every EdelYarn piece begins in our Zurich fitting studio. A trained stylist takes thirty plus precise measurements, discusses your fabric, lining, button and lapel choices, and confirms every detail before a single thread is cut.

No guesswork. No mail-in tape measures. Just a careful, unhurried hour with someone whose only job is making sure your garment fits you, and you alone.

Vietnamese craft

Crafted in Vietnam.

Your specifications are sent to our atelier partners in Hoi An and Ho Chi Minh City, families who have been hand-tailoring suits and dresses for three generations. They cut your pattern by hand, baste the canvas, and stitch the lapels with techniques rarely seen outside of Savile Row.

The result is a garment with the soul of a heritage craft and the precision of a Swiss appointment, at roughly a third of the price you'd pay on Bond Street.

What we stand for

Three principles that shape every decision we make.

Honest pricing

No markup for the name on the label. You pay for the fabric, the fit and the hours, nothing else.

Material matters

We work only with mills whose wools, silks and linens we'd happily wear ourselves, and we tell you exactly where they come from.

Made to last

A well-cut, well-stitched garment should outlive trends. Ours are built to be altered, re-lined and worn for decades.

Our process

How it works

From your first browse to a finished garment on your doorstep, a transparent four-step journey, guided by Swiss expertise and crafted by master tailors in Vietnam.

1

Choose your piece

Browse our tailored suits and custom dresses. Pick the silhouette and fabric direction that fits your style and your occasion, there's no wrong starting point.

2

Book a measurements fitting

Schedule a 60-minute in-person session at our Zurich studio. We'll confirm fabric choice, take precise measurements, and discuss any styling preferences.

3

Swiss-guided, Vietnam-crafted

Your specifications are sent to our trusted master tailors in Vietnam. They hand-cut and stitch your garment using premium wools, silks, and linens.

4

Delivered to your door

Within 3–5 weeks, your finished garment arrives carefully packaged. Final payment is taken on delivery, only after you've confirmed the fit and finish.
